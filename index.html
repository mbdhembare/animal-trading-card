What's Your Favorite Animal?

Cat

Cat have a unique ability to land on their feet when they fall, a skill known as the "righting reflex." This reflex allows cats to rotate their bodies in mid-air and orient themselves so that their feet hit the ground first.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: Between 18 to 24 inches in body length, excluding the tail, which can add an additional 9 to 10 inches
  • Habitat: Adaptable and can live in a variety of environments
  • Lifespan: 12 to 15 years

The domestic cat (Felis catus) is a small, typically furry carnivorous mammal known for its playful and curious demeanor. As one of the most popular pets worldwide, cats are recognized for their adaptability to various living environments, from compact apartments to spacious homes with gardens. They are independent animals requiring low-maintenance care, making them appealing to many pet owners.
